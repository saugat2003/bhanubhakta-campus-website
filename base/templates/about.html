Introduction

Bhanubhakta Campus, located in the scenic Shuklagandaki-4, Tanahun, stands as a beacon of higher education excellence in Nepal. Named after the revered poet Bhanubhakta Acharya, often called the "Adikavi" (first poet) of Nepal, our campus carries forward his legacy of knowledge, culture, and enlightenment.

Established with the vision of providing accessible and quality higher education, we have been serving the educational needs of students from Tanahun and surrounding districts. Our campus is committed to fostering academic excellence, research innovation, and character development among our students.

Key Highlights:

  • • Comprehensive bachelor's degree programs
  • • Experienced and qualified faculty
  • • Modern infrastructure and facilities
  • • Strong industry partnerships
  • • Active research and innovation culture
